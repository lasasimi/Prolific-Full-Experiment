What is the purpose of this study?

The purpose of this study is to investigate how people form opinions in groups. Results from this study will help us understand collective phenomena such as polarization and consensus. You have been invited to participate in this study because you are at least 18 years of age.

What is the procedure of the study?

You will be participating in an online research experiment involving personal opinions and group discussions. The study consists of three phases:
üìò Phase 1 ‚Äì Training & Comprehension, 2 minutes. You will first go through a training session to understand the task, followed by a comprehension quiz to ensure everything is clear.
üó≥Ô∏è Phase 2 ‚Äì Survey, 5 minutes. You will be asked to read four dilemmas that a community might encounter regarding different social or political issues, and indicate your opinion about each. These first two phases are completed individually, with no interaction with others.
üí¨ Phase 3 ‚Äì Group Discussions, max. 5 minutes. If you pass the comprehension quiz and complete the opinion questions, you will be able to participate in a group discussion about one of the dilemmas with 9 other participants. Initially, you may need to wait up to 10 minutes for a group to form. There will then be several short rounds of discussion, each with a new subset of 4 other participants. In each round, you‚Äôll see what these people think about the dilemma.

What are the benefits of participating in the study?

Your participation will help us understand how people form opinions in groups. You will also be compensated for the duration of the study according to standard hourly rates.

Your participation in this research study is voluntary. You may refuse to participate without penalty at any time. If you decide to participate, you are free to stop at any time without penalty by just stopping and/or telling the investigator. If you complete the study but later decide that you want your data removed, please contact the study coordinators at collectivemindslab@csh.ac.at.

How will the data collected in this study be used?

The personal data collected from you is the minimum relevant to the scope of the present study, and it will only be used for the specific, explicit and legitimate purposes for which they are obtained. Data on your actions during your participation will also be collected and used for research purposes. Once your data has been fully anonymized and cannot be linked back to you in any way, it will be analyzed and placed in an Open Data Repository to be shared with other researchers. This will be done solely for research purposes and not for commercial use, in compliance with EU policy. Aggregated data may be shared to support reproducibility of published results. Personal data will not be used for purposes that are incompatible with the purposes for which the data were collected. Personal data will be stored on our servers until you lodge an objection or withdraw your consent. The data will not be transferred outside the EU. The personal data collected is not subject to automated decision-making, and not subject to profiling.

Will there be any costs for the participants? Will they receive reimbursement or remuneration?

There is no cost for taking part in the study. The study consists of three phases, where the first two are completed individually, with no interaction with others. You will be compensated for the duration of the study according to the standard hourly rate. You will receive a base payment of 1.40 USD only if you fully complete both Phase 1 and Phase 2.
