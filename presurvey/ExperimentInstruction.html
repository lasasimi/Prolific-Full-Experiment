🟨 Phase 2: Survey

Welcome to Phase 2: Survey
In the next phase, you will read a series of dilemmas encountered by your village. After each dilemma, you will be asked a set of questions. Before we begin, we will show you the neighborhood situation again to help you recall the context.

✅ You must complete this phase, along with Phase 1, to receive the base payment.

🏘️ Imagine that you live in a village of around 50 houses owned by neighbors of different ages, occupations, and incomes.

👨‍👨‍👦‍👦 The village has many shared facilities and sometimes issues arise about what to do collectively about these facilities.

🏡 There are often different opinions on how to handle these issues. All neighbors meet as a community once every six months to make a decision about the neighborhood problems.

💭 We’d like to hear your initial opinions on the dilemmas your neighborhood is facing, which will be introduced on the next screen.
