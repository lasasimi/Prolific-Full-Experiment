🟦 Phase 1: Training & Initial Survey

Welcome to Phase 1

Before we begin, we will give you a short training session to familiarize you with the task. The next screen will show you an example of a dilemma and you can see how your neighbors responded to it. You will be given a chance to respond to the dilemma and then answer some questions about it to test your comprehension.

✅ You must complete this phase to be eligible for payment and to continue in the study.

🏘️ Imagine that you live in a village of around 50 houses owned by neighbors of different ages, occupations, and incomes.

👨‍👨‍👦‍👦 In a village where people share spaces and responsibilities, issues arise about how to make shared decisions in the best interest of the community and get along together.

🏡 There are often different opinions on how to handle these issues. All neighbors meet as a community once every six months to discuss issues in the neighborhood to reach a decision.

💭 We’d like to hear your initial opinions on the dilemmas your neighborhood is facing, which will be introduced on the next screen.
