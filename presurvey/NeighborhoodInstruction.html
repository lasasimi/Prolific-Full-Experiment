🟦 Phase 1: Training & Initial Survey

Getting to know the task

Before we begin, here is a short training session to get to know the task.

🏘️ Imagine that you live in a village of around 50 houses owned by neighbors of different ages, occupations, and incomes.

👨‍👨‍👦‍👦 In the village, people share spaces and responsibilities, and issues arise about how to make shared decisions in the best interest of the community and get along together.

🏡 There are often different opinions on how to handle these issues. All neighbors meet as a community once every six months to discuss issues in the neighborhood to reach a decision.

💭 We’d like to hear your initial opinions on the dilemma your neighborhood is facing.
